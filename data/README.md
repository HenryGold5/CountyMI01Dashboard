The following folder contains all the data needed to run the dashboards.
**SHAPEFILES AND GEOPACKAGES**
- district1Presincts.gpkg: GeoPackage of all the precincts within Michigan District 1. Includes voting results for
  each precinct.
- district1Overall.shp: Shapefile of the whole of District 1.
- district1Metro.shp: Shapefile of the outlined Metro areas within all of District 1.
- district1Counties.shp: Shapefile of all the counties within District 1.

**EXCEL AND CSV FILES**
- votingResultsCountyWide.xlsx: Voting Results for each county in MI-01 for Congress, Senate, President, and
  Governor from 2024-2014.
- voterTurnout2024.xlsx: Total voter turnout for each country for the 2024 election.
- spendDataVA.xlsx: VA Spend for each county for the most current year of available data.
- numEmployeesCounty.xlsx: County-wide employee numbers by category of employer.
- numEstablishments.xlsx: County-wide number of establishments by category of employer.
- payRollCounty.xlsx: County-wide total payroll by category of employer.
- CountyDemographics.xlsx: County-wide demographic totals by various demographics.
    - Sheet 1: Age and Sex for Population Pyramids.
    - Sheet 2: Type of College Degree for those with a College Degree in the county.
    - Sheet 3: Highest educational attainment level.
    - Sheet 4: Household income levels.
    - Sheet 5: Median Income of the county.
    - Sheet 6: Hispanic Latino percentage within the county.
    - Sheet 7: Race breakdown by county.
